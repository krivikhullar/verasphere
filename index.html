ETL & Data Pipelines

Your data lives in five different places and none of them talk to each other — Shopify, QuickBooks, a Google Sheet, your POS, your email platform. We build the plumbing: automated pipelines that move, clean, and consolidate your data into one place, reliably, every single day.

What you get at the end of the engagement:

  • Automated data pipelines with error alerting
  • Consolidated data in a clean central store
  • Scheduled refresh (daily, hourly, or real-time)
  • Data quality checks built in
  • Monitoring dashboard so you know when something breaks
  • Full handoff documentation
